Prices and Services

We offer two different services, visits at home and stays at our facility.

If you want a home visit we come to your house clean and feet the cat. Usually in that case you would provide the food and other necessities for your cat.

If you want to bring your cat to our facility this is also a possibility. We have room and plenty of enrichment for our feline guests. In that case we can provide food and necessities for the cat. Of course you can still bring the food and other necessitis for your cat espacially if it has special needs.
